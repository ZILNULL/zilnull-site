# Hi

Welcome to my site!

I made this website to throw all my writing into a single place. Things will probably be a little messy, but that's just how I roll. I'll post thoughts on things I'm reading or playing, along with random mini-essays or just straight up rambles. I tend to like writing streams of consciousness a lot, and I don't really have a place where I can throw them.

Maybe I'll even post stories about my characters here, or some other stuff. I don't know. It's going to be chaos. That's kind of the point. (Except, this time, the chaos isn't inundating your timeline.)

Use the side bar and feel free to look around!
